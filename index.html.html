FAQ

Common Questions

How do I make a reservation?+
Simply call or text (360) 927-0408, or email thescottsdaledriveclub@gmail.com. We'll confirm availability and walk you through the process — no complex portals.
What documents do I need?+
A valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a major credit card. We'll walk you through everything before pickup.
Is there a minimum age?+
Drivers must be at least 25 years of age with a clean driving record. Please confirm eligibility before booking.
Are prices all-inclusive?+
Daily rates are clearly stated and we'll discuss deposit, mileage, and terms before you confirm. No surprises at pickup.
Can I rent for multiple days?+
Absolutely. Multi-day rentals are available and often preferred. Contact us to discuss availability and extended arrangements.
What if I get in an accident?+
We'll go over insurance requirements and damage policies before your rental. You'll need to carry your own coverage. Everything is explained clearly before you take the keys.
Do you offer delivery?+
Reach out directly to discuss delivery options. We're flexible and aim to make the process as convenient as possible.
How far in advance should I book?+
At least 24–48 hours in advance. Weekend and holiday availability goes fast, so earlier is always better.
